## Layout & Spacing
- **Base Spacing**: 4px
- **Container Padding**: 24px
- **Card Gap**: 16px
- **Section Margin**: 40px
- **Gutter**: 16px
- **Border Radius**: Small (4px), Default (8px), Medium (12px), Large (16px), Extra Large (24px), Pill (9999px)

## Components & Visual Identity
- **Floating Cards**: White background (#FFFFFF) with a 1px soft border and subtle ambient shadow. 20px uniform corner radius.
- **Buttons**: Pill-shaped (circular ends). Primary CTA is filled with Dark Green (#0D3D35) and white text. Secondary uses Light Green (#DCEEEA) with Dark Green text.
- **Inputs**: Minimalist 1px border that becomes Dark Green on focus. Rounded 16px. Small labels above fields.
- **Chips/Tags**: 500px radius, subtle background fill, no border. Used for Priority and Category.
- **Icons**: Lucide-style 2pt stroke icons. Only filled when active/selected in nav bar.
- **AI Elements**: Marked with a subtle gradient border transitioning from Dark Green to Light Green.
